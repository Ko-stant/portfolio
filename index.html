About me

For many years I had been working on troubleshooting consumer hardware and software. And every day I made use of several websites to research how to resolve new problems that new technologies inevitably brought.

Over the years I ran into the good, the bad, and the unusable websites. I began working with a friend to start building my own websites, and while the progess was initially slow I enjoyed every minute of it.

Because of this I took an opportunity to enroll in an immersive program to quickly develop my newfound passion into a career.
